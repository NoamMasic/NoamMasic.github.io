What is an FAQ Page?

An FAQ page, short for Frequently Asked Questions page, is a webpage on a website that provides answers to commonly asked questions about a particular topic, product, or service. It helps users find quick solutions to their inquiries, saving time and improving user experience.



Why do you need an FAQ page?

An FAQ page is beneficial because it helps address common concerns and questions that users may have, reducing the need for individual customer support inquiries. It improves user satisfaction, saves time for both users and businesses, and can serve as a valuable resource for self-service support.



Does it improves the user experience of a website?

Yes, an FAQ page can significantly improve the user experience of a website. By providing readily accessible answers to common questions, it saves users the time and effort of contacting customer support or searching through multiple pages for information, resulting in a more efficient and satisfying browsing experience.
